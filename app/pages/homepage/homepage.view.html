We are pleased to introduce the new Performance Management System for the employees of Archirodon.

Performance Management is an annual process that links employees and jobs to the strategy of the organization and establishes a shared understanding of what has to be achieved and how. It is a tool that monitors and ensures that employees are doing the right things, in the most effective ways, and to the best of their ability.

Performance Management aims to:

  • Clarify what is expected of employees and how they can contribute to the success of their team.
  • Encourage an ongoing dialogue between employees and their direct supervisor / Manager throughout the year.
  • Evaluate and measure the performance of employees in a fair, objective and consistent manner.
  • Provide balanced feedback and guidance to employees.
  • Provide employees with opportunities for professional development, growth and career advancement.
  • Recognize good performance and enhance the motivation of employees.
  • Align company and individual performance, helping all to work in the same direction.

ARCOPM is the online application to help you complete the Performance Management.

In case you require assistance please use the Support menu icon on the left-hand side.

Also, please use the Help menu icon on the left-hand side to see a step-by-step guide on how to set goals and complete the performance evaluations.
